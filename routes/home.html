Futurama Quotes API


"Fetch some quotes why not?" - Dr. John Zoidberg


Data format:

{name: "characters name", quote: "the quote", score: "1-3 difficulty to guess"}

Routes

"/" - You are here

"/playgame" - A game of "who said what" in Futurama

"/randomquote" - Retrieves one randomly selected quote

"/allquotes" - Retrieves an array of all the quotes
